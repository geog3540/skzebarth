Geovisualization: GEOG 5540

Thomas Fire Debris Flow Basin Modeling

This Observable notebook observes the USGS Preliminary Hazard Assessment (PHA) for the Montecito PFDFs. Basin area is normalized to assess risk density across basins. The goal of this excercise is to use USGS modeling data and observe new methods of understanding PFDF risk.

Graduated Symbol Mapping with in-situ Chlorophyll-a

This Observable notebook creates graduated symbol mapping with in-situ chlorophyll-a values obtained from Big Spirit Lake in Okoboji, Iowa. Chlorophyll-a was collected on 9/11/2023 and lab-verified with the Lakeside Laboratory State Hygenic Lab extension.

Johnson County Bivariate Mapping

This Observable notebook uses 2020 Census data at the census tract level for bivariate mapping of average Household Size and percentage renter occupied for households in Johnson County, Iowa.

Flowmapper

This project uses Geo-Social lab migration flow data from 1887 - 1924 to derive the directionality of past human movements throughout the United States.

Final Project Proposal

Here, I propose to use satellite remote sensing to predict chlorophyll-a in Big Spirit Lake in Iowa with PlanetScope, Sentinel-2, and Sentinel-3 for 9/11/2023 with in-situ chlorophyll-a verified by the State Hygenic Laboratory.
